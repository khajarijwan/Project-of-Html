Information About Fitness:

Bycycle Training

1.As a beginner, ride 2–3 times per week for a total of 2–3 hours.
2.Shorter rides take 24 hours or less to recover from, but high-intensity rides require 24–48 hours of recovery time.
3.Cyclists of all skill levels should get at least one full rest day per week and the day before a Fondo.

Sports Training

Working out at the gym regularly keeps you from getting hurt when playing sports because your muscles have been built up and trained to handle the type of exertion competitive sports demand. Every sport requires strength in specific muscles, making it worth your while to have them in shape
